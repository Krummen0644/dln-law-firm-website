    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I pay in cash?

    Yes, you can pay in cash at our office during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM). Please bring your invoice or reference number so we can properly apply your payment.

    How do I get a receipt?

    After your payment is processed, you'll receive a confirmation email from the payment provider. We'll also send you an official receipt from our office within 1-2 business days.

    What if I don't know my Case ID?

    Your Case/Reference ID should be on any invoice or billing statement we've sent you. If you can't find it, please contact our billing team at (859) 555-0199 or billing@example.com.

    Is my payment information secure?

    Yes. We don't collect your credit card or bank information directly. All payments are processed securely through trusted third-party providers (PayPal, Venmo, Square, or Cash App), which use industry-standard encryption.

    Can I set up a payment plan?

    Absolutely. If you need to arrange a payment plan, please contact us directly. We're happy to work with you to find a solution that fits your situation.

    How long does it take for my payment to be applied?

    Payments are typically applied to your account within 1-3 business days. If you included the memo/reference information, it helps us process it faster.
