Criminal Defense FAQs

Should I talk to the police?

No. Politely decline to answer questions and immediately request an attorney. Anything you say can and will be used against you, even if you think you're helping your case. Exercise your right to remain silent and call us at (859) 555-0100.

How much does a criminal defense attorney cost?

Fees vary based on case complexity. We offer free consultations to discuss your case and provide transparent pricing. We also offer payment plans to make quality representation affordable. The cost of not having an attorney is far greater.

What if this is my first offense?

First-time offenders may be eligible for diversion programs, deferred prosecution, or reduced sentences. We'll work to keep your record clean and explore every option for a favorable outcome.

Can I get my charges dismissed?

Possibly. We've successfully gotten charges dismissed due to lack of evidence, procedural errors, illegal searches, witness credibility issues, and constitutional violations. Every case is unique—contact us to evaluate your situation.
