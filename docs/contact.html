Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a consultation cost?

Your initial consultation is completely free. We'll discuss your case, answer your questions, and explain your options at no charge and with no obligation.

How are your fees structured?

Fee structures vary by case type. For personal injury and medical malpractice cases, we work on a contingency fee basis (no fee unless we win). For criminal defense and family law, we charge flat fees or hourly rates. We'll explain all costs upfront during your consultation.

How long will my case take?

Case timelines vary greatly depending on complexity. Some cases settle quickly, while others may take months or years. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

What should I bring to my consultation?

Bring any relevant documents: police reports, medical records, court papers, contracts, photos, witness information, and a written timeline of events. Don't worry if you don't have everything—we can help you gather what we need.

Do you handle cases outside Northern Kentucky?

Yes! While we're based in Covington and primarily serve Northern Kentucky, we represent clients throughout the Commonwealth of Kentucky.
